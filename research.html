Economic Integration Agreements and Agricultural Trade Margins
Trade agreements reshape agricultural trade by expanding the range of exported products, making diversification a central source of trade growth. These diversification effects are stronger for processed goods, high-income partners, and deeper agreements, and continue to build over time.
